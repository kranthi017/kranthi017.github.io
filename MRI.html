MRI Image Registration and Segmentation

In this project, I developed an algorithm that will automatically
segment a brain MRI scan into anatomical regions of interest. For the sake of simplicity, I only worked on mid-coronal Slices.

For this project, I had access to some MRI scans, few of which had manual segmentation given while the task was to find the segmentation for the rest. Splitting the task, initially I had to register the given training images using four parameters: (global scale, rotation, and translations along two axes). Once I registered the training images over the given two validation images I then registered the segmented images of the same scans. Finding the mode of the six segmented images gave a final automatic segmented image whose accuracy was measured using Jaccard index wrt the validation images which were manually segmented.

Once the functions were finalised, I then proceeded to register the training + validation images wrt the testing images given. The registration parameters were then used to transform the segmented images and using various fusion methods, I then segmented the transformed images. Multiple segments accuracy was then measured with the manually segmented testing images. Further details of the project can be found here.
