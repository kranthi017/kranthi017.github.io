About me

I am Master's graduate from Cornell Univesity (Ithaca, NY, US) in Electrical and Computer Engineering, and before that
I graduated from Shiv Nadara University (Dadri, UP, India) with Bachelor's, majoring in Electronics and Communication Engineering and minor in Management.
Since Sophomore year, when I first found out about microcontrollers, I have been constantly exploring the depth and breadth of embedded systems. I have a keen interest for understanding and developing solutions using embedded systems for everyday problems. Here, in this website, you will find few such projects of mine exploring various microcontrollers and SOC's.
